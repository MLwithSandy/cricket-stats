Team India

 The India men's national cricket team, also known as Team India and Men in Blue,[11] is governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). India had been granted the status of ICC Full Member in 1932 and they played their inaugural Test match at Lord's in June.

 From 1932 India had to wait until 1952, almost 20 years for its first Test victory. In its first fifty years of international cricket, India was one of the weaker teams, winning only 35 of the first 196 Test matches it played.

 Traditionally much stronger at home than abroad, the Indian team has improved its overseas form, since the start of the 21st century, winning Test matches in Australia, England and South Africa. They are also one of the only four teams to win all major ICC.

 As of July 4, 2021, India is ranked second in Tests, fourth in ODIs and second T20Is by the ICC.
