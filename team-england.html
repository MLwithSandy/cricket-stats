Team England

 The England cricket team represents England and Wales in international cricket.

 As of 18 July 2021, England have played 1,036 Test matches, winning 377 and losing 309 (with 350 draws).

 In Test series against Australia, England play for The Ashes, one of the most famous trophies in all of sport, and they have won the urn on 32 occasions.

 As of 20 May 2021, England are ranked third in Tests, fourth in ODIs and first in T20Is by the ICC.
