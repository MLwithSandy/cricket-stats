Team West Indies

 The West Indies cricket team, nicknamed the Windies, is a multi-national men's cricket team representing the mainly English-speaking countries and territories in the Caribbean region and administered by Cricket West Indies. The current side represents:

  • Antigua and Barbuda
  • Barbados
  • Dominica
  • Grenada
  • Guyana
  • Jamaica
  • Saint Lucia
  • Saint Vincent and the Grenadines
  • Trinidad and Tobago
  • Parts of Saint Kitts and Nevis
  • Anguilla
  • Montserrat
  • British Virgin Islands
  • Sint MaartenL
  • US Virgin Islands
