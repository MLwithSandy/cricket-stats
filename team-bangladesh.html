Team Bangladesh

 The Bangladesh men's national cricket team, popularly known as The Tigers.

In November 2000, it played its first Test match against India in Dhaka, becoming the tenth Test-playing nation.

 Bangladesh holds the record for most consecutive losses in Tests (21, between 2000 and 2002) and ODIs (23, between 2001 and 2004). After gaining Full Member status with the ICC, Bangladesh had to wait until 2004 for its first ODI win since the 1999 World Cup.

 As of 18 July 2021, Bangladesh has played 124 Tests, winning 15.

 As of 18 July 2021, Bangladesh is ranked 9th in Tests, 7th in ODIs and 10th in T20Is by the ICC.
